Turkey Chili

Description

This turkey chili recipe is very simple but hearty with plenty of flavors - it's even better on the second day! I serve this chili with low-fat Cheddar cheese and low-fat sour cream. It's also delicious with crackers.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground turkey
  • 1 large white onion
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can of kidney beans
  • 1 tablespoon of chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon of paprika
  • 1 tablespoon of dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon of cayenne pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of cumin
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook onions in olive oil until onions are translucent.
  2. Add ground turkey too the onions and cook until browned.
  3. Add garlic to the turkey and onion mixture, and cook until fragrant.
  4. Add in the crushed tomatoes, kidney beans, and spices.
  5. Let it come to a boil, then reduce the heat, cover, and let simmer for 30 minutes.
